# One Perfect Day in Athens

## Morning: Acropolis & Parthenon (08:00–11:00)

Start early at the **Acropolis** (UNESCO World Heritage Site) to beat the crowds and heat.
Walk up from Dionysiou Areopagitou Street — the path takes about 15 minutes from the base.

- **Parthenon**: The iconic temple of Athena, under ongoing restoration.
- **Erechtheion**: Known for its Porch of the Caryatids — six female statues as columns.
- **Odeon of Herodes Atticus**: Ancient theatre still used for performances (check schedule).

Entrance: €20 adults, €10 reduced (2026 prices; verify on-site). Combined ticket with other
archaeological sites available. Allow 1.5–2 hours inside.

**Transport**: 10-minute taxi from hotel (€7–9). Alternatively, walk 25 minutes through Plaka.

## Mid-Morning: Acropolis Museum (11:00–13:00)

Just below the Acropolis, the **Acropolis Museum** houses original sculptures and artefacts.
The glass floor reveals ancient ruins beneath. Don't miss the Parthenon Gallery on the top floor.

Hours: Tue–Sun 09:00–20:00 (last entry 19:30). Closed Mondays.
Entrance: €15 adults.

## Lunch: Plaka / Monastiraki (13:00–14:30)

Walk down through **Plaka** — Athens' oldest neighbourhood, full of neoclassical houses
and souvenir shops.

- **Café Avissinia** (Monastiraki Flea Market): rooftop views, traditional mezedes.
- **Tzitzikas & Mermingas** (Mitropoleos St): excellent modern Greek taverna, €15–25/person.
- **Hoocut** (near Syntagma): quick gourmet souvlaki wrap, €6–8.

## Afternoon: National Archaeological Museum (14:30–17:00)

One of the world's great archaeology museums. Highlights:
- **Mask of Agamemnon** (Mycenaean gold).
- **Antikythera Mechanism** — earliest known analogue computer.
- Cycladic figurines and Ancient Egyptian collection.

Hours: Tue–Sun 09:00–20:00. Entrance: €15 adults.
Address: 44 Patission St (28 Oktovriou). Taxi from Monastiraki: 15 min, ~€8.

## Sunset: Lycabettus Hill (17:30–19:30)

The highest point in Athens with a 360° panoramic view. Take the **funicular railway**
(€7 round trip) or hike up (30 min). The view over the Acropolis at sunset is spectacular.

- Small café at the summit. Bring water.

## Evening: Dinner in Kolonaki / Koukaki (20:00)

- **Aleria** (Koukaki): creative modern Greek cuisine, mains €18–28. Reservation recommended.
- **To Kafeneio** (Kolonaki): upscale traditional Greek, great wine list.
- **Nolan** (Vouliagmenis Ave): fusion Greek-Asian, vibrant atmosphere.

After dinner, explore the bars of **Monastiraki** and **Psirri** for live music and cocktails.
