# Athens Family Day — Fun for Kids & Adults

## Option A: Beach Day at Glyfada (All Day)

**Glyfada** is Athens' most accessible beach resort, ~18 km from the hotel.

- **Astir Beach**: organised beach with sunbeds, watersports, and a children's pool. €12 entry.
- **Yabanaki Beach**: more family-friendly, free public section available.
- **Getting there**: Taxi from hotel ~30 min, €23–27 (or tram Line 5 in ~45 min).

Pack sunscreen (strong Greek sun even in spring). Beach equipment rentals on-site.
Seafood tavernas line the Glyfada promenade for lunch.

## Option B: Athens Zoo — Attica Zoological Park

Located in Spata (near the airport), Attica Zoo is Greece's largest.

- Over 2,000 animals; popular with children of all ages.
- **Highlights**: white lions, penguins, gibbon forest, bird sanctuary.
- Hours: Daily 09:00–17:00 (summer 09:00–19:00). Entrance: €19 adults, €14 children (3–12).
- **Getting there**: Taxi ~40 min, €45–50 (return trip recommended for families with strollers).

Allow 3–4 hours for a full visit. Café and picnic areas on-site.

## Option C: Athens with Kids — City Highlights

### Morning: Hellenic Children's Museum
Interactive hands-on exhibits for ages 3–12. Located at 14 Kydathinaion St, Plaka.
Hours: Tue–Fri 10:00–14:00, Sat–Sun 10:00–15:00. Entrance: €3.

### Afternoon: Stavros Niarchos Foundation Cultural Centre (SNFCC)
A modern landmark with open lawns, a canal, and a children's library.
Free admission. Great for running around. Canal paddleboats available (€6/20 min).
**Getting there**: Taxi ~20 min, €16–20.

### Evening: Syntagma Square Changing of the Guard
The **Evzones** (presidential guard) perform a ceremonial change every hour at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier, right next to the hotel. Children love watching in their traditional uniforms. Free, daily.

## Family Dining Recommendations

- **Diporto** (Theatrou Sq): old-school taverna, generous portions, kid-friendly.
- **Bel Ray** (Glyfada): relaxed seafood and pizza by the beach.
- **Flocafé / Everest** chain: reliable Greek fast-food bakeries, good for quick bites with kids.

## Tips for Families

- Stroller-friendly: Syntagma, the waterfront promenade, and the SNFCC. The Acropolis is NOT stroller-friendly (cobblestones and steps).
- Baby equipment: ask the front desk — the hotel has cots, high chairs, and a list of nearby pharmacies stocking formula.
- Emergency: nearest children's hospital — Aglaia Kyriakou, 15 min by taxi.
